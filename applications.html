Mwogo, Rukarara & Kavili Dam Sites - Rwanda

Infrastructure / Energy Rwanda 2025

Integrated geophysical and geotechnical investigation for three critical dam sites in Rwanda. Deployed advanced seismic refraction and MASW techniques for comprehensive subsurface characterization and dam foundation analysis.

Project Outcomes

  • Completed 1200m seismic refraction surveys across all three sites
  • Conducted 1200m MASW surveys for shear wave velocity profiling
  • Integrated geophysical data with geotechnical investigations
  • Delivered comprehensive dam foundation suitability assessment

Energy / Mining Burundi 2024

Comprehensive geophysical and geotechnical investigation for a pioneering 1MW hydro-solar hybrid power facility serving Anzana Electric/Songa Energy operations in Burundi, combining renewable energy sources for a sustainable energy mix for Mutambu, Burundi.

Hybrid Energy Solution

  • Characterized foundation conditions for hybrid power facility
  • Integrated hydropower and solar energy infrastructure assessment
