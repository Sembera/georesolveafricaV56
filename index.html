Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our mineral exploration and geoscience services in East Africa

Do you provide mineral exploration services in Uganda?

Yes. GeoResolve provides comprehensive mineral exploration services across Uganda and East Africa, including geophysical surveys, geological mapping, pitting and trenching programs, and RC and diamond core drilling management. We hold an active exploration license in Uganda and have delivered projects for clients throughout the region.

What geophysical methods are used for mineral exploration?

We deploy ground magnetics, induced polarization (IP), electromagnetics (EM), electrical resistivity tomography (ERT), and gravity surveys for mineral exploration across East Africa. For regional-scale targeting, drone magnetic surveys cover large areas efficiently before detailed ground follow-up.

Can you log and manage exploration drill core?

Yes. Our geologists provide professional RC chip and diamond core logging with strict QAQC protocols, using our proprietary G-ResoLog digital borehole logging system. We manage drill programs from collar surveying and core orientation through sampling, dispatch, and database compilation for projects across Uganda, Rwanda, and Burundi.
