Kenya Leads Africa's Direct Air Capture Revolution

October 2025 Climate Tech & Innovation 8 min read

Kenya has emerged as a global leader in direct air capture (DAC) technology, with Octavia Carbon commissioning Project Hummingbird - the first commercial DAC plant on the African continent. The East African Rift Valley's unique combination of abundant geothermal energy and basaltic rock formations provides ideal conditions for carbon removal and permanent storage, positioning the region to potentially contribute 20% of global carbon dioxide removal by 2035.
